OpenAI refers to the organization behind models like ChatGPT, which focuses on developing advanced AI technologies for various applications. Claude, on the other hand, is a conversational AI model developed by Anthropic. While both aim to provide AI-driven conversational capabilities, they differ in their underlying architectures, training methodologies, and organizational philosophies. OpenAI emphasizes safety and alignment in AI, while Anthropic focuses on building AI systems that are interpretable and aligned with human values.
