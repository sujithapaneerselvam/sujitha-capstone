Prompt engineering is the process of designing and refining input prompts to effectively communicate with AI models, particularly language models. It involves crafting specific questions or statements to elicit desired responses, improve accuracy, and enhance the relevance of the output. Key aspects include:

1. **Clarity**: Ensuring the prompt is clear and unambiguous.
2. **Context**: Providing sufficient background information to guide the model.
3. **Specificity**: Being precise about what information or format is needed.
4. **Iterative Testing**: Experimenting with different prompts to find the most effective wording.

Effective prompt engineering can significantly improve the performance of AI applications in various domains, such as content generation, question answering, and more.
